Key Insights

  • Highest Risk Department: Sales department shows 20.6% attrition rate
  • Critical Roles: Laboratory Technicians (62), Sales Executives (57), and Research Scientists (47) account for 70% of attrition
  • Age Factor: Employees aged 26-35 show highest turnover (116 departures)
  • Income Correlation: Employees earning under $3,000/month have 25%+ attrition rate
  • Overtime Impact: Employees working overtime are 3x more likely to leave (31.5% vs 10.4%)
